I am a Master’s student in Robotics at Purdue University and a Graduate Student Researcher in the MARS Lab under Prof. Yu She, focusing on learning-based manipulation and tactile sensing. My research develops intelligent manipulation systems through imitation learning, diffusion policies, and multi-modal sensory integration — with current work on tactile-enhanced manipulation for contact-rich tasks and cross-modal generative models for embodied AI.

I have built vision-guided manipulation systems on UR5/UR5e arms with RealSense depth cameras, implementing inverse kinematics and real-time path planning for industrial automation. My technical work spans deep learning (PyTorch), simulation (Isaac Gym, PyBullet, CoppeliaSim), and real platforms (Franka, UR5e, LEAP Hand), alongside ROS/ROS2 development and computer vision with OpenCV — end-to-end, from hardware integration to intelligent control. Earlier, I designed multirotor drone platforms at IIT Bombay’s e-Yantra lab.

Zhiyuan Zhang, Adeesh Desai, Jyun-Chi Hu, Yosuke Saka, Quan Khanh Luu, Jiuzhou Lei, Davood Soleymanzadeh, Bihao Zhang, Minghui Zheng, Yu She

Projects

ROS-Based Motion Planning & Trajectory Execution for Robotic Manipulation
2025

Implemented a ROS-based motion-planning pipeline converting Cartesian end-effector trajectories into time-parameterized joint-space plans via forward and inverse kinematics. Discretized continuous task-space trajectories (Lissajous curves) into 500 Hz joint trajectories compatible with real-time ROS controllers, enforcing joint limits, sampling-rate, and smooth position/velocity constraints. Validated planned end-effector paths in RViz and deployed on physical robot hardware for a full planning-to-execution workflow.

Grocery Store Automation
2025

An autonomous robotic system for grocery automation — restocking empty shelves and returning misplaced products. Mounted a UR5 manipulator on a Pioneer3DX mobile robot (AMR) for pick-and-place, using depth-camera vision for 3D coordinate extraction and object recognition, inverse kinematics for manipulation, and path planning for collision avoidance. Multi-process integration synchronized AMR navigation with manipulator control to demonstrate inventory retrieval, shelf navigation, product placement, and misplaced-item repositioning.

Vision-Guided Sorting using UR5e

A complete vision-guided sorting system on the UR5e arm. Developed Python/OpenCV pipelines integrated with an Intel RealSense D415 depth camera for real-time object sorting and orientation tracking, enabling pick-and-place and visual servoing. Programmed via ROS for 5-DOF pick-and-place and 1-DOF visual servoing with inverse-kinematics and smooth trajectory planning, validated in both CoppeliaSim and on real hardware.
